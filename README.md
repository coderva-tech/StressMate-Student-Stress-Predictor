### Project Description
StressMate is an interactive student stress predictor designed to help you understand, track, and manage your stress levels effectively. By analyzing key lifestyle and academic factors such as sleep quality, study load, headaches, academic performance, and extracurricular activities, StressMate provides a personalized stress assessment to support your mental wellness journey.

### The Problem Statement
Students today face unprecedented levels of stress due to academic pressures, irregular sleep schedules, heavy workloads, and the challenge of balancing multiple responsibilities. Many students lack effective tools to identify their stress levels early and take preventive action, leading to burnout, poor academic performance, and mental health issues. This project addresses the need for accessible, data-driven stress prediction.

### The Solution
StressMate leverages data analysis and machine learning to predict stress levels based on quantifiable lifestyle factors. This interactive web application collects user input about sleep quality, study hours, health symptoms, academic grades, and extracurricular involvementâ€”then generates a personalized stress assessment with actionable recommendations for stress management and mental wellness.
